Introducing Global Venture Network

Revolutionizing decentralized finance with our comprehensive VST ecosystem

  • About GVN

    The Global Venture Network (GVN) represents a paradigm shift in decentralized finance, introducing a comprehensive ecosystem built around the Venture Share Token (VST). Our platform combines traditional investment principles with cutting-edge blockchain technology, creating sustainable wealth generation opportunities for a global community.

  • Vision And Mission

    To democratize wealth creation by establishing the world's most accessible, transparent, and rewarding decentralized financial ecosystem, empowering individuals globally to participate in the new economy regardless of their background, location, or initial capital.

  • What is Smart Contract?

    Our smart contracts leverage EVM-compatible architecture, ensuring interoperability while maintaining security through comprehensive auditing processes. The platform incorporates strong measures, multi-signature wallet security, and transparent on-chain tracking of all transactions and reward distributions.

  • Why Choose VST?

    VST offers multiple income streams through staking, referrals, team bonuses, and rank rewards. With a fixed supply of 80 million tokens and sophisticated 15-Level reward system, GVN provides 2x-5x growth potential based on investment Levels while maintaining mathematical sustainability.

01- What is VST (Venture Share Token)?

VST (Venture Share Token) is a DeFi system built on BEP-20 blockchain with a total supply of 80 Million tokens. Launch price is $1 with potential monthly growth of 10% to 30%, and listing price expected between $10 to $100.

  • Token Name: Venture Share Token (VST)
  • Blockchain: BEP-20 (Binance Smart Chain)
02- What are the 4 Types of Income in VST?

VST offers 4 different income streams: Staking ROI (0.6% to 0.9% per 12 hours), Team Incentive (10% to 1%), Instant Bonus (5%), and Rank and Reward system with monthly rewards up to $10,000.

  • Staking ROI: 0.6% to 0.9% per 12 hours
  • Team Incentive: 10% to 1% on 15 Levels
03- What are the VST Staking Plans?

VST offers 2 staking plans: Star Staking ($100-$10,000) with 0.6% ROI every 12 hours, and Smart Bond Staking ($100-$10,000) with 0.6%-0.9% ROI every 12 hours and auto-compounding for 90, 180, and 360 day terms.

  • Plans: Star Staking & Smart Bond Staking ($100 - $10,000)
  • Principal amount anytime withdrawal
04- What are the Key Points of VST?

VST is a 100% decentralized project on international platform with $10 minimum withdrawal, 5% deduction on withdrawal, no locking of funds, and unlimited income potential with no capping on single ID.

  • $10 minimum withdrawal, 5% deduction
  • 100% Decentralized international platform
05- What are the Rank and Rewards in VST?

VST has 10 rank Levels from Entrepreneur ($100 self, $5000 team, $10 monthly) to Supreme Tycoon ($10,000 self, $10,00,000 team, $10,000 monthly). Each rank offers progressive monthly rewards and team bonuses.
